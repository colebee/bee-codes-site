About Fitness Roguelite

Fitness Roguelite is an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) mobile app that gamifies your fitness journey. By integrating with wearables like Garmin, it turns real-world activities—such as steps, workouts, and heart rate data—into procedural rogue-like adventures. Explore dungeons based on your daily steps, battle mobs with resistance training stats, and recover with rest data.

This app is in early development as a prototype to validate the concept, with plans for commercial launch. For privacy details, see our Privacy Policy.
