Privacy Policy for Fitness Roguelite

Last updated: 07/16/25

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bee Codes ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile app, Fitness Roguelite ("the App"). The App is a fitness gamification tool that integrates with wearables like Garmin via APIs to enhance your experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable laws, including GDPR and CCPA. If you have questions, contact us at cole@bee.codes.

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Account Information: Email, username, and password for registration.
  • Fitness Data: Steps, heart rate, workouts, and activity details synced from Garmin APIs.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS, and unique identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, session duration, and gamification progress.

How We Collect Your Data

Data is collected:

  • Directly from you during account creation or manual input.
  • Automatically via app usage and device sensors.
  • Through third-party APIs like Garmin, with your consent.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide gamified fitness features (e.g., generating dungeons from steps).
  • Personalize content and improve the App.
  • Provide support and communicate updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) bound by confidentiality.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.
  • No third parties for marketing without consent. We do not sell your data.

Data Security

We use encryption, access controls, and regular audits to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Opt out of data processing or withdraw consent.
  • Request data portability.
  • For CCPA: Know what data is collected/sold, opt out of sales (we don't sell data).
  • For GDPR: Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities.

Email requests to cole@bee.codes. We respond within 30-45 days.

Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed for the purposes described (e.g., active accounts) or as required by law, then securely delete it.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data, we delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to servers outside your country (e.g., US for cloud services). We use safeguards like standard contractual clauses to ensure protection under GDPR/CCPA.

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

For EU users (GDPR): We process data based on consent or legitimate interests. You have enhanced rights as noted.

For California users (CCPA): We disclose categories of data collected/shared. No sales occur, but you can opt out if applicable.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes are posted here with the updated date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
